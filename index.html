About Us

DEcadence? DeCaDANCE? DeKAWdence? No matter what you call us, DeCadence ("duh-KAY-dence") is a student-run, co-ed a cappella group from UC Berkeley that loves to add some music and fun to your day. We sing indie, pop, jazz, and any other genre that comes our way, often while sporting our iconic gold capes.



Events

We're currently on summer break, but when the Fall 2018 semester starts, we've got lots of fun things planned! Come see us perform at the UC Berkeley Welcome Back A Cappella Showcase on Friday, August 24th! More details to come.

Auditions

Come audition for us this coming semester! Check out our auditions page for more info. For additional questions, contact our auditions coordinators Jess Bonnar and David Mai at jlbonnar@berkeley.edu and damai@berkeley.edu

Want to Find Us?

Come see us perform every Wednesday at noon under the beautiful Sather Gate! If you can't make it, you can watch us on this Sproul livestream , like us on Facebook, double tap us on Instagram, or check us out on YouTube!
