Hi, I'm Rain Lehmkuhl! (he/they)

I'm a second year PhD student in the PDOS and CSS groups at MIT working under the excellent guidance of Henry Corrigan-Gibbs in the areas of cryptography, computer security, and systems. I am supported by an MIT Sunlin and Priscilla Chou Fellowship and an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

Before beginning my PhD, I worked at Opaque, a startup that provides a platform for collaborative data analytics on private data.
