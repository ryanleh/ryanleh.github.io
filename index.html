Hi, I'm Ryan Lehmkuhl!

I also go by Rain (he/they)

I’m a fourth-year PhD student at UC Berkeley, advised by Henry Corrigan-Gibbs, working in cryptography, computer security, and systems. I’m interested in using cryptography to improve auditing techniques for technology like chatbots and recommendation systems.

The first three years of my PhD were done at MIT. Before beginning my PhD, I worked at Opaque, a startup that provides a platform for collaborative data analytics on private data. Prior to that, I graduated with a BS in EECS from UC Berkeley under the advisement of Raluca Ada Popa, Alessandro Chiesa, and Pratyush Mishra.
